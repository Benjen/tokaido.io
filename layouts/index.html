Get Started View on Github

Tokaido is still in alpha release. You should expect to encounter at least some bugs and problems.

Lightweight

Tokaido uses Docker so it runs quick and with minimal system resources. The average Tokaido environment uses less than 300MB of RAM

Fast

Tokaido is the fastest way to provision Druapl dev environments. Most Tokaido environments launch in less than 15 seconds.

Collaborative

Simple and easy to manage configuration files can affect just your local instance, or be committed and shared with your colleagues

Powerful

Tokaido runs out-of-the-box, but can be customised in incredible detail to provision the perfect environment for your site, again and again.

Production-quality

Tokaido environments are replicas of production-grade Docker containers, running with sane defaults for security, caching, and performance.

Open Source

Tokaido and the containers it runs are free, open source softwared. When we're out of Alpha, we'll release the code, but it's not quite ready yet.
